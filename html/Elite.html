The Elite Platoon

Qualify ☆ Compete ☆ Win

TTOC is known for its competitive atmosphere. This season, we've included a new bracket where only the best of the best can compete in. This competition consists of a different set of rules; be sure you understand them!

Elite Platoon Championship

  • Classic gamemode with all the weapons and bullets.
  • A round begins when Referee tanks are destroyed.
  • Round Robin style bracket.
  • Total of 15 rounds, fight with all you've got!
  • Top 2 players will receive an exclusive accessory, not even crew members will have it!
  • The winner earns the title of Champion, and is featured at the bottom of this page.
  • Keep an eye out for the Elite Championship thread.

Bracket Information

Each player will compete against seven other players. You will play the same player twice; once in your favorite server, and once in your opponent's favorite server. In the event of unmatchable times, be prepared to be flexible. By accepting the Wild Card, you acknowledge these rules.

How to Qualify

This is an 8 player bracket. Within the season, you must place in the Top 2 of any official gamemode. Placing in the Top 2 earns you a Wild Card. The player may decline the Wild Card(s), but this does not pass the Wild Card slot down to the next available player. The Wild Card will be unused.

Wild Card

It is possible for a player to earn multiple Wild Cards throughout the season. If a pre-qualified player earns a Wild Card, the Wild Card will be passed on to the next highest ranked player, who is not already qualifed. If the player chooses to decline the Wild Card, the Wild Card will be unused.

Match Info

This is a round robin style bracket. All players will be matched twice. Rankings will be determined by total points earned in all matches. Please be keen on the schedule. A time and server will be posted below, unless otherwise noted. If a match does not have a qualifying time, be cooperative and work out a time.

Wild Card Advancement

Wild Cards are awarded according to the order below

Order Sort Criteria
1st Top 2

3rd 4th place
